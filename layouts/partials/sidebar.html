YOUR MOVE MATTERS

Lorna Jane Clarkson has been advocating Active Living for over 20 years and launched Active Nation Day in 2012 – A day that stops the nation and asks the question "Are we doing enough?"

Since 1980, worldwide obesity has more than doubled while 31% of the world’s population languishes with insufficient exercise. And Heart disease has become the leading cause of death worldwide.

Active Nation Day on Sunday September the 25th is your chance to do something – show your family, friends and the world that Moving Matters to you.
