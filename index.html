BECAUSE MOVING MATTERS

We all know it’s important to move our bodies but so often we find it hard to make it happen. Since 1989, Lorna Jane Clarkson has been advocating her philosophy of Active Living- Move, Nourish, Believe. Her mission has always been to inspire women to put their health and fitness first in order to live their best active lives. But with worldwide obesity more than doubling since 1980, Lorna had to ask the question ‘Are we doing enough?’. Her answer to this was to raise awareness through Active Nation Day.

Choose to move your body, choose to put your health and fitness first. Show your friends, your family and the world that moving matters! Join us this Active Nation Day and let’s get moving!
